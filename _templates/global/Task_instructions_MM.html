On your screen, you will see 16 blue squares. Each square represents a hidden card which can be revealed by clicking on it. Your task is to find as many matching pairs of cards as possible within the time limit.

The task will rely on your mathematics skills.

üïπÔ∏è How to play

  1. Click on a card to flip it over and see what's on it.
  2. Click on another card to flip it over.
  3. If the two cards match, they will disappear.
  4. If they do not match, they will flip back over and be hidden again.
  5. Repeat the above to find as many matching pairs in the time.

The rule for matching pairs depends on the difficulty level of the cards.

Easy level
Each easy card shows an addition problem.
Two cards match if their solutions are the same.
